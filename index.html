Biography

Aggrey Ongweso is a Kenyan Data Analyst and Software Developer with experience in research data management, statistical analysis, and health informatics. His work focuses on transforming complex datasets into actionable insights that support evidence-based decision-making in public health and research.

He has worked with the Kenya Medical Research Institute (KEMRI) under the Health Demographic Surveillance System (HDSS) and the Population-Based Infectious Disease Surveillance System (PBIDS), contributing to data analytics, statistical modeling, database management, and quality assurance.

“Using statistics, research, and technology to improve health systems and drive data-driven solutions across Africa.”

Ongweso is also experienced in backend software development using C#, VB.NET, SQL Server, RESTful APIs, SOAP services, and database-driven applications. He has contributed to the design and implementation of data collection systems using ODK and REDCap.
